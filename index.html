I think about us exploring new places together. Stumbling into streets we've never seen. Finding little cafes and trying everything on the menu. Getting a little lost and not caring at all. Taking photos of everything — the places, the food, each other — and building an album of memories that belongs only to us.

I think about the quiet, intimate moments too. The ones that don't need a caption or a memory — just the two of us, close, unhurried, present. The kind of moments where nothing needs to be said because everything is already felt. Your head on my shoulder. My hand finding yours without thinking. Just being together — completely, peacefully, yours and mine.

I think about being there for every version of you. The happy you. The tired you. The excited you. The you that lights up over dessert. The you that worries too much about everyone else. The truth is, Sweety — I don't know exactly what our future looks like. But I know you're in it. And somehow, that makes every part of it feel like something worth looking forward to.

You are not just my present, Sweety. You are every future I ever want to have.

❤️

Forever and always yours,
